About Us

Located in Eau Claire, Badger Wordsmith, LLC is a small, discount marketing firm. Our talented staff focuses on assisting small-to-medium sized businesses by developing affordable marketing products and services.

Badger Wordsmith, LLC works in partnership with our clients to:

  • Develop lists for direct mailing initiatives
  • Implement and manage the Saleable Seven ™ drip marketing program
  • Graphically design and print brochures, post cards, and fliers
  • Design advertisements for newspapers and other periodicals
  • Produce written copy for Web sites, essays, brochures, and white papers
  • Draft and disseminate news releases for businesses, individuals, and organizations
  • Provide a variety of consulting services
  • Edit books, articles, and other correspondence
  • Fashion and create business logos
  • Cover news or sporting events by using stringer services
  • Sell and distribute golf shirts, t-shirts, sweatshirts, jackets, and ball caps bearing business logos and/or other designs
  • Cold call or follow-up with prospective customers
  • Operate marketing campaigns from conception to completion
  • Create slogans for companies or organizations.

At Badger Wordsmith, LLC, our business model is predicated on controlling costs.

Unlike some of our competitors, BWS doesn’t spend several thousand dollars a month leasing trendy office space or rent expensive luxury boxes from professional sports franchises.

Our goal is to provide cost effective marketing solutions to entrepreneurs seeking to grow their businesses. In some instances, Badger Wordsmith, LLC’s prices are up to 70 percent less than other marketing or advertising companies. So why pay more and get less? Plus, the BWS customer service model is second to none. As a small company, there are no phone trees to navigate. A call to Badger Wordsmith, LLC is answered or returned by a quality representative.
