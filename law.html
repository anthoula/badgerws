Law Enforcement Report Writing

If you are interested in a career as a law enforcement officer, but have little policing experience, Badger Wordsmith, LLC (BWS) is here to lend a helping hand.

During the testing process, many law enforcement agencies require applicants to draft a narrative report.

BWS represents a consortium of veteran-law enforcement officers, many of whom are currently retired. At your request, BWS will dispatch an instructor to present an eight-hour law enforcement report-writing seminar.

BWS provides individual or group sessions.

Make no mistake about it, the narrative report you author will have an impact on the overall hiring process. With the economy in tough shape, competition for law enforcement positions has never been greater. Get a leg up on other applicants by contacting BWS.

BWS does require the sponsoring party to supply the necessary meeting space and a Power Point projector.
